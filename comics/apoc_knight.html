Apocalypse Knight (2024)

Story: Eidon Betz

Art: Levi Comics

APOCALYPSE KNIGHT follows the story of Dave Tanner, a home inventor who finds a new life after a nuclear apocalypse. Believing he is the only one equipped with the knowledge, skills, and morals to survive the wastelands, Dave takes it upon himself to become the defender of his town. Unbeknownst to him, there are sinister forces looming to seize power in the aftermath of this world shattering event. Forces that may be closer to home than he realizes.
